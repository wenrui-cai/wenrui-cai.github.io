High School Students' Grades

In a team of 5 students, we built a model of secondary school students' grades in relation to different variables. Through Regression and Variance analysis tools we found the best predictors of final grades for these students. We also analyzed specifically whether alcohol consumption was a significant predictor of final grades. From our analysis, as alcohol consumption increases, if considered alone, grades decrease, but it is not relevant if other variables are considered.
