About Me

Hello and welcome to my Website! Here I share my personal information about my passions, goals, and, in general, about my lift. I was born and raised in Milan, Italy in a Mandarin-speaking family. Thanks to the rich education I received, I became fluent in English and I was able to continue my higher education in the United States.

Thanks to an enlightening experience in researching about Math Education at University of Auckland (New Zealand) and teaching a recitation class of Business Calculus at Penn State, I became passionated about Math Education. I would like to become a life-long educator, so for this purpose, I am pursuing a Masters in Math Education at Teachers College - Columbia University. Outside out classes, I find myself enjoying traveling, hanging out with friends, and learning languages.

Work Experience

Math Tutor Peer Coordinator- Penn State Learning (Jan 2018 - Present)

I am currently a Peer Coordinator for Penn State Learning, a position that I enjoy at my fullest. I interview new candidates, guide new tutors, and constantly give feedback to the current tutors on how to improve their tutoring skills. In addition, I also lead a team of 10 tutors to create math review packets executed on LaTex which are approved by the math department. Ever since January of this year, Penn State Learning has become my new "home": my passion for leadership along with my love for helping people, has been motivated me to commit myself entirely to this position.

Math Tutor- Penn State Learning (Jan 2016 - Present)

At Penn State, I tutor students in all levels of maths, from algebra to advanced calculus and statistics. I enjoy tutoring one-o-one better, but often, I assist groups working on the same topics and give review sessions to up to 200 students. What do I like about tutoring? The connection I make with the students, where math is our language in common. I also enjoy meeting many other tutors who I share in common the passion toward this subject, from which I learn different skills on a daily basis.
